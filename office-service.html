Venture Setup

Register your buisness

If you are starting a new business, determining the type of business entity is crucial. Should you go with a sole proprietorship, partnership, OPC, LLP, or Pvt. Ltd.?
Our services include guiding you on choosing the right business entity, assisting with necessary registrations and certificates, ensuring compliance with legal requirements, providing expert advice on taxation rules and strategies, facilitating access to government subsidies and grants.
This support is not only beneficial for entrepreneurs but also for students and salaried individuals interested in starting their ventures.
At Zartaj Solutions, we are here to help you at every step of your business journey. Whether you are just starting or looking to expand, our goal is to provide comprehensive support to help you succeed.

Sole Proprietorship

Perfect for individual businesses with minimal complexities. Unlimited liability for the owner.

One Person Company

Suitable for single entrepreneurs looking for limited liability. Only one director is required.

Partnership Firm

Ideal for businesses with multiple owners. Partnership deed governs the agreement.

LLP (Limited Liability Partnership)

A hybrid structure combining features of a partnership and a company. Limited liability for partners.

Public Limited Company

Suitable for businesses planning to go public. Shares traded on the stock exchange. Stringent regulations.

Private Limited Company

Offers limited liability for shareholders. Suitable for medium to large-sized businesses.

Joint Venture

Collaborative business arrangement between two or more parties for a specific project or goal.
